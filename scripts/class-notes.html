Concept

Exploration of population dynamic patterns at The Portal Project. How do counts of rodents like Dipodomys species change through time?

In this document I will:

  1. Load data from the Portal Project Teaching Database
  2. Process it into population time series
  3. And make initial visualizations

Analysis

Get the time-series of counts for all species.

time_series <-
  data %>%
  group_by(species_id, year) %>%
  summarize(count = n()) %>%
  filter(species_id %in% c('DM', 'DO', 'DS')) %>%
  na.omit()
